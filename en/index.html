The Aquablasting Process & Use Cases

What is Aquablasting?

A wet-blasting process using a water and glass bead slurry - gentler than dry sandblasting, ideal for achieving a uniform satin finish without damaging parts.

How it works & benefits

The slurry is pumped onto the surface, lifting contaminants while preserving shape and dimensions. This closed-loop, low-dust method is cleaner, creates less waste, and is perfect for precision cleaning of soft metals like aluminum.
